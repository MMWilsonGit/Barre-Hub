Barre Neighbors Fund · Managed by Barre Up

Every neighbor deserves a chance to be flood-ready

The Barre Neighbors Fund distributes money on a first-come, first-served basis according to need and available funding. Applications are reviewed and funds distributed by Barre Up. Donate to the fund, or apply below if you need help covering the cost of resilience improvements for your property.

🌿 Native plants & rain gardens

🤝 Community planting days
🏠 Backwater valve assistance

How it works: Applications are reviewed by Barre Up on a first-come, first-served basis. Questions? Write to us at info@barreup.org
